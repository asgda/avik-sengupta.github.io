Research Focus

During my PhD at IIT Hyderabad, I develop computational approaches to colorectal cancer theranostics, integrating transcriptomics, genomics, Hi-C, and clinical data from TCGA/DepMap cohorts. My work spans drug repurposing, prognostic modeling, and multi-omics subtyping.
